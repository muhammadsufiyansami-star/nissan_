TITAN Key Features

The 2024 TITAN lineup redefined towing, delivering impressive strength and capability with a gooseneck hitch rating of up to 11,050 lbs, handling a wide range of payloads. TITAN XD Crew Cab 4X4 Shown.

The PRO-4X had a locking rear differential for enhanced traction, burly tires for extra grip, and skid plates for armor underneath. PRO-4X was a serious off-road package with the off-road gauge and hill descent control.

The TITAN Lineup featured the acclaimed Endurance V8, with 400 @ 5,800 rpm Horsepower and 413 Lb-ft of Torque which offered more than enough power to tackle tough terrain or big jobs.

Vehicle Differences
What is the difference between the 2024 Nissan TITAN and TITAN XD?
The TITAN is a full-size pickup, while the TITAN XD is a heavy-duty pickup with higher towing and payload capacities. The XD offers enhanced off-road capability and more rugged features for demanding work and adventure.
