Nissan Ariya Overview

Design & Performance

The Nissan Ariya is an all-electric crossover SUV that combines futuristic design with advanced technology and performance. It features a "Timeless Japanese Futurism" design, available with two battery options (63 kWh or 87 kWh) and two drivetrain configurations (front-wheel drive or e-4ORCE all-wheel drive).

Battery & Charging

The Nissan Ariya is available with two battery options: a 63 kWh (or 67 kWh) and an 87 kWh battery. Both use a liquid-cooled thermal management system for consistent performance in different climates and support fast charging capabilities.

Road Grip & Stability

The Nissan Ariya offers excellent road grip, especially with the optional e-4ORCE all-wheel-drive (AWD) system. It uses dual motors to intelligently distribute torque between the front and rear wheels for maximum traction and control.

Interior Comfort

The Nissan Ariya is designed with comfort in mind, featuring a spacious, lounge-like interior with high-quality materials, a flat floor, and "zero gravity" front seats for superior relaxation during long drives.

Car of the Year 2022

The Nissan Ariya was named Auto Express Car of the Year 2022, praised for its comfortable ride, superb refinement, and agility — particularly impressive for a large car.
