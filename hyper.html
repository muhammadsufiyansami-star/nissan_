Nissan Hyper Tourer Experience

The Future of Premium Mobility

The Nissan Hyper Tourer concept is an all-electric minivan focusing on nurturing and reinforcing the bond between people as they journey together. The concept is geared toward individuals who appreciate the finer things in life and enjoy the company of friends and associates, whether on a road trip or business outing.

Your Fine Driving Experience Awaits

The Nissan Hyper Tourer combines the essence of omotenashi (Japanese hospitality) with various advanced technologies, including autonomous driving and V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) functionality.

The exterior, which conveys a sense of comfort within, is composed of smooth body panels and sharp character lines that demonstrate traditional Japanese beauty while providing high aerodynamic performance for a smooth driving experience. The signature lamp headlight and kumiko-patterned wheels create a premium feel beyond the concept's class.

Experience Japanese Futurism

Nissan's EV Technology Vision creates a spacious interior by consolidating compact components and all-solid-state batteries. Nissan's advanced e-4ORCE technology and the vehicle's ultra-low center of gravity evoke speed, comfort, and smooth driving.

The interior features an LED floor, 360-degree floating seats, and bio-sensing technology to ensure a pleasant travel experience.

Seamless Autonomous Comfort

The autonomous vehicle enables those in the driver's seat to focus on interacting with their travel companions. The seats can swivel 360 degrees, allowing front- and rear-seat passengers to have face-to-face interactions.

Passengers in the rear can use a wearable display to view and operate navigation and audio, creating a sense of unity and shared experience among all occupants.
