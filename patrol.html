THE LEGACY OF NISSAN PATROL

Unstoppable Strength. Iconic Capability.

Engineered for Power

The Nissan Patrol stands as a legend of capability. With its powerful V8 engine and commanding presence, it delivers unmatched performance on highways and rugged landscapes alike.

Premium Interior Comfort

Inside the Patrol, premium craftsmanship meets modern convenience. Spacious seating, refined materials, and advanced connectivity create a high-end environment for every journey.

All-Terrain Capability

Equipped with an advanced 4x4 system, the Patrol adapts instantly to challenging terrains. From sand dunes to mountain trails, its intelligent power distribution ensures maximum confidence and control.

Advanced Safety Technology

The Patrol integrates Nissan Intelligent Mobility features such as Intelligent Emergency Braking, Lane Intervention, and Intelligent Around View® Monitor, enhancing awareness and safety on every drive.

Nissan Safety Features

Nissan Safety Features

ProPILOT: A first for the Patrol, Nissan’s intuitive and user-friendly driving assistant, the ProPILOT, is designed with you in mind. It takes control of steering, braking, and accelerating, all while ensuring you stay confidently centered in your lane. Your convenience and safety are at the heart of this ultimate co-pilot.

Rear Automatic Emergency Brake: Reverse with complete control. The Rear Automatic Emergency Brake is designed to keep an eye out for moving objects when you’re reversing, and if necessary, takes immediate action by automatically applying the brakes and alerting you to obstacles, ensuring your safety.

Predictive Forward Collision Warning: When you are on the road, sensors will be on the lookout for what is ahead. Predictive Forward Collision Warning with the Patrol’s laser range finder will alert you with visual and audible warnings to potential collisions.
