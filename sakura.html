NISSAN SAKURA

The Nissan Sakura can refer to two things: the Nissan Sakura electric car, which charges using standard or quick charging methods, or the "Sakura" brand of car battery chargers, which are for 12V batteries. The electric car can fully charge in 8 hours using standard charging or reach 80% charge in about 40 minutes with quick charging.
