Nissan 350Z Engine

Nissan 350Z (5th Generation) Engine

The fifth-generation Nissan Z, known as the 350Z (Z33), was powered by a 3.5-liter VQ35DE V6 engine delivering around 287 horsepower and 274 lb-ft of torque. Later versions like the 350Z Track and 350Z NISMO models used the upgraded VQ35HR engine, pushing output to 306 horsepower. This naturally aspirated V6 engine became famous for its sharp throttle response, strong mid-range pull, and legendary reliability — making the 350Z a modern classic among driving enthusiasts.

Nissan 350Z Drift Achievements

Nissan 350Z — The Drift Legend

The Nissan 350Z has earned its reputation as one of the most iconic drift cars in motorsport history. With its powerful V6 engine, perfect weight balance, and rear-wheel-drive setup, it has dominated drift competitions worldwide. Drivers around the globe have pushed the 350Z to its limits, securing countless trophies and proving its supremacy on the track. From D1 Grand Prix to Formula Drift, the 350Z continues to be celebrated as a true drifting icon.

350Z — Master of Control

Precision and balance define the 350Z’s dominance on the drift circuit. Its rear-wheel-drive dynamics and responsive steering provide unparalleled control, making it a top choice for professional drifters and enthusiasts alike. Whether carving through tight corners or powering through long slides, the 350Z’s engineering perfection makes every drift a masterpiece of motion.

Nissan 350Z Body Strength

Strong and Rigid Body Structure

The Nissan 350Z was engineered with an exceptionally rigid body structure designed to enhance handling, safety, and overall performance. Its lightweight yet strong chassis is built from high-tensile steel and reinforced aluminum components, providing superior torsional rigidity. This solid foundation allows the suspension to perform with precision, ensuring stability at high speeds and confidence through tight corners. The result is a sports car that not only looks aggressive but feels incredibly planted and durable on any road.
