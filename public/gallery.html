NISSAN HERITAGE TIMELINE

1958 Mobilgas Trial (Round Australia)

Year: 1950

Category: Motorsports

Nissan demonstrated its durability and endurance by participating in the 1958 Mobilgas Trial across Australia — one of the world’s toughest rallies.

1963 The 1st Japan GP

Year: 1960

Category: Motorsports

The first-ever Japan Grand Prix marked the beginning of Nissan’s high-speed motorsport dominance, setting the stage for decades of racing excellence.

1966 The 14th East African Safari Rally

Year: 1960

Category: Motorsports

Nissan vehicles proved their legendary reliability in Africa’s extreme conditions, finishing the world’s most demanding rally with distinction.

1971 The 19th East African Safari Rally

Year: 1970

Category: Motorsports

Building on its rally heritage, Nissan’s performance cars continued to conquer terrains worldwide, marking the brand’s strong off-road presence.
