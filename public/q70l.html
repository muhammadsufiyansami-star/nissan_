Driving Force

INFINITI Q70 L Performance

The Q70 L comes with two engine options: a 3.7L V6 producing up to 330 hp, and a 5.6L V8 producing 420 hp. Variable Valve Event and Lift Technology (VVEL®) ensures instant engine response for seamless performance.

Polished and Poised

INFINITI Q70 Design

The INFINITI Q70 exterior was beyond refined. Its sweeping proportions and seductive lines made drivers look twice. Inside, available semi-aniline leather seats surrounded you in luxury. For added comfort, the Q70L added 5.6 inches of legroom to the rear row. The same attention to detail lies at the heart of both the Q50 and QX50.

INFINITI Q70 Design

Style That Shines

Magnetic down to the very last detail, the Q70 offered a distinguished look. Its signature double-arch grille boasted an intricate diamond-mesh pattern with chrome accents. Two eye-inspired LED headlights added dimension to the exterior, while enhancing visibility. With more signature details, the Q50 and QX50 are raising the bar even higher.

INFINITI Q70 Rims

Luxury In Revolution. The INFINITI Q70 looked poised from crown to toe. With available 20-inch split 5-spoke aluminum alloy wheels, this sedan grabbed your attention. Factor in the available all-season performance tires, and it could grab hold of the road, too. Now the Q50 and QX50 offer a selection of wheel designs to complement your personal style.

INFINITI Q70 Safety

Power to Command

The INFINITI Q70 enhanced your awareness and control. It offered the Around View® Monitor with Moving Object Detection to help you park. Plus, it put a range of Driver Assist Technologies in your hands, to help you avoid hazards. These included Blind Spot Intervention, Predictive Forward Collision Warning, and many more. Discover the evolution of INFINITI safety.

INFINITI Q70 Features

With You All the Way
The INFINITI Q70 offered a touch-screen navigation system to help direct you. With voice recognition and 3D building graphics, it was intuitive for any driver. It also helped reduce stress on the highway, indicating which lane to take in time for each exit. Discover more advanced navigation options in the new Q50 and QX50.

Symphonic Sound
The right soundtrack enriches each journey. That's why the INFINITI Q70 offered Bose® Studio Surround Sound. This available 16-speaker system immersed you in high-fidelity audio. With Bose® Centerpoint® signal processing, every frequency sounded more present. Experience Bose® audio and enhanced connectivity in the Q50 and QX50.

The QX70 Lives On

INFINITI Vehicles

The INFINITI Q70 left an undeniable legacy. While this iconic sedan is no longer in production, you can find the same powerful elegance in the Q50 and QX50. We invite you to take a closer look for yourself.
