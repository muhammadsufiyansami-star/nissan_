Nissan N7 Concept

The Nissan N7 is a next-gen electric sedan introduced in 2025. It combines futuristic technology with elegant design, starting at just $16,600. Equipped with a Snapdragon processor, a 15.6-inch infotainment screen, and intelligent driver assistance, the N7 leads Nissan’s global EV lineup.
