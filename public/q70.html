Intelligent All-Wheel Drive

The INFINITI Q70 provided all-season control: both the V6 and V8 versions were available with Intelligent All-Wheel Drive. This sent power to select wheels based on the terrain. When roads were slick, it diverted up to 50 percent of the power to the front wheels for better traction.

Q70 Sport

A sport package was available for both the V6 and V8 versions. Along with sport-tuned suspension, they had high-contrast carpet and headliner, unique front seat designs, sport steering wheel and shift knob stitching, and aluminum-trimmed pedals.

Blind Spot Intervention

The Q70 was available with Blind Spot Intervention® — an innovative driver assist system. It provided selective braking if your vehicle approached an adjacent lane and another vehicle was detected in the blind spot area. The system would intervene and help the driver return the vehicle back toward the center of the lane.
