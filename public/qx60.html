Designed with families in mind and luxury at its core, the INFINITI QX60 blends refined comfort with bold presence. Every detail — from its elegant silhouette to its spacious, tech-forward interior — elevates your driving experience.

Luxury Meets Capability

The INFINITI QX60 delivers confident performance through its powerful V6 engine, advanced all-wheel drive, and a suite of intelligent technologies designed to make every journey seamless.

Confidence on Every Road

With Intelligent All-Wheel Drive and Drive Mode Selector, the QX60 adapts seamlessly to conditions — ensuring composed, capable handling whether in the city or off the beaten path.
