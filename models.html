About Nissan Concept Cars

Nissan’s concept cars represent more than design — they express a vision for the future of mobility. From electrification to autonomous technology, each concept showcases bold creativity and cutting-edge innovation. These designs embody Nissan’s commitment to pushing boundaries, creating vehicles that blend emotion, performance, and sustainability.

Every concept model — from the Hyper series to the timeless Z and GT-R — captures Nissan’s philosophy of “Timeless Japanese Futurism.” With a focus on advanced electric platforms and driver-centric experiences, Nissan continues to redefine what the future of driving feels like.
